Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.
  • Device information — browser type and operating system, collected automatically via server logs.

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is a free tracking tool and does not process financial transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To provide and maintain your account and expense records.
  • To respond to support requests or inquiries you send us.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.
  • To understand how features are used, in aggregate, to guide improvements.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

If you delete your account, your personal data and expense records will be removed from active systems within a reasonable timeframe. Some data may be retained in backups for a limited period to comply with legal obligations or to resolve disputes.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data only as needed to deliver their services and may not use it for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with external advertising networks, analytics platforms that track you across sites, or social media SDKs that collect personal data.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a session cookie to keep you logged in between page loads. This cookie contains no personal information beyond a session identifier and is deleted when you log out or when your session expires.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may do this through your account settings or by contacting us directly. You also have the right to export your expense data in a portable format upon request.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the application's support channels. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
